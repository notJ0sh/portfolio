From Player to Creator.

My journey into tech started with a simple goal: I wanted to create games that others could enjoy. While I hit roadblocks early on, a school program using Scratch reignited my passion, leading me to take programming as a subject in Secondary 3 and get an A for my O levels.

I spent my early years self-teaching Python, HTML/CSS, JavaScript, and Java. This foundation allowed me to return to Roblox Studio with a new perspective—mastering Luau to build successful, experience-driven games rather than just following tutorials.

Today, I’ve shifted my focus toward Software Engineering and AI. I've moved from solo projects to team-based hackathons and industry-standard workflows. I am constantly learning, with my sights set on SUTD to further refine my craft.
