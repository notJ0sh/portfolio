From player to creator

My journey into tech started with a simple goal: I wanted to create games that others could actually enjoy. I hit some roadblocks early on and almost gave up, but a school programme using Scratch reignited my passion. That led me to take programming in Secondary 3, where I eventually bagged an A for my O Levels.

Before returning to game development, I spent time teaching myself the fundamentals of Python, HTML/CSS, JavaScript, and Java. Building this solid foundation was the "missing piece" I needed—it allowed me to finally head back to Roblox Studio with a proper understanding of how code actually works. I moved past copy-pasting and mastered Luau to build successful, experience-driven games from scratch.

Today, my focus is on Software Engineering and AI. I've traded solo projects for team-based hackathons and industry-standard workflows. I’m always learning something new, with my sights firmly set on SUTD to keep refining my craft.
